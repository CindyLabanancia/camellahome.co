House Details

The company's history can be traced back to 1977 when the company was established by Manny Villar, who is now a well-known businessman and politician in the Philippines. At that time, Camella Homes focused on building affordable homes in the suburbs of Manila. Over the years, the company has expanded its reach to other parts of the country, and today it is one of the largest and most trusted home builders in the Philippines.

Camella Homes offers a wide range of home options that cater to the needs and preferences of different homebuyers. Their homes come in different sizes, styles, and designs, ranging from townhouses to single-detached houses. The homes are built using high-quality materials and are equipped with modern amenities to ensure comfort and convenience for its homeowners.

Your Dream Life Awaits in Camella

Camella Homes offers include spacious bedrooms, well-designed kitchens, and cozy living rooms. The homes are also equipped with modern fixtures and fittings, such as air conditioning units, water heaters, and built-in cabinets. Additionally, most Camella Homes communities offer amenities such as clubhouses, swimming pools, parks, and playgrounds to promote an active and healthy lifestyle for its residents.

In terms of location, Camella Homes has communities in various parts of the Philippines, including Metro Manila, Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. The company has a reputation for building homes in strategic locations that are accessible to schools, hospitals, malls, and other essential establishments.

Vista Land

Vista Land is one of the largest and most successful real estate developers in the Philippines. The company was founded by Manny Villar in 1977 as a homebuilding company called Camella Homes. Over the years, the company grew and expanded its operations, eventually becoming Vista Land in 2007. Today, Vista Land is a publicly-traded company that has built over 400,000 homes and has a presence in over 147 cities and municipalities in the Philippines.

Vista Land's success can be attributed to its focus on building quality and affordable homes for Filipinos. The company offers a wide range of home options that cater to different income brackets, from affordable housing to luxury homes. Vista Land also offers financing options that make it easier for Filipinos to own a home of their own.

Vista Land Life made Beautiful

In addition to building homes, Vista Land also develops and manages commercial properties such as malls, hotels, and office buildings. The company's commercial properties include the Vista Mall, which is a chain of malls that offer a wide range of shopping, dining, and entertainment options.

Vista Land's commitment to excellence has earned it numerous awards and recognitions. The company has been named as the "Best Developer" by various organizations and has been recognized for its contributions to nation-building and sustainable development.
