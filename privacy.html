Privacy Policy

Introduction

The privacy policy of Camella Homes outlines how the company collects, uses, and protects the personal information of its customers, website visitors, and other individuals who interact with the company. Camella Homes is committed to safeguarding the privacy of all its stakeholders and ensuring that personal data is processed in compliance with relevant laws and regulations.

1.1. Sharing of Personal Information

Camella Homes may share personal information with third-party service providers who assist with payment processing, marketing, and other business functions. Camella Homes does not sell or rent personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Camella Homes may also share personal information with government authorities or law enforcement agencies in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

1.2. Security of Personal Information

Camella Homes takes appropriate measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. Camella Homes implements physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal information.

2. Collection of Personal Information

Camella Homes collects personal information from customers and other stakeholders when they inquire about or purchase properties, register for newsletters or promotional materials, apply for employment, or interact with the company in any other way.

The types of personal information that Camella Homes may collect include:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Financial information (credit card information, bank account details, payment history)
  • Property preferences (location, type of property, budget)

Your Rights

Camella Homes respects the rights of individuals to access, correct, and delete their personal information. Individuals may also object to the processing of their personal information for certain purposes. Camella Homes will respond to such requests in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Changes to Privacy Policy

Camella Homes may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the company's practices or legal requirements. Camella Homes will notify stakeholders of any material changes to the privacy policy and provide them with an opportunity to review and comment on the changes.

Conclusion

Camella Homes is committed to protecting the privacy of its stakeholders and ensuring that personal information is processed in compliance with applicable laws and regulations. Camella Homes values the trust of its stakeholders and strives to maintain transparency and accountability in its data processing practices.
